Built a Notification Sytem for Alerting

THE PROBLEM

During my time as an integration specialist at Derivco I had some challenges finding a way to centralize our notifications and alerting. The issue was not the fact that we did not have alerting in place, but rather the alerts were all decentralized and made maintenance and updates cumbersome and time consuming.

THE SOLUTION

I then decided to tackle this problem hands on in my spare time to build an API that could handle all alerts regardless of its origin. I then added two modules to the notification system (Email & Teams). The purpose of these modules would be to send the alerts to a DL or Teams Channel based off a configuration file.
