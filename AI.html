Used Accord .Net AI framework for Visual Regression

THE PROBLEM

We had mobile games launching in an iframe. This however, presented us with an issue where we were unable to interact with any of the objects within the iframe. The objective was to be able to determine at the very least if the game launched successfully.

THE SOLUTION

I decided to have a look at image comparison solutions. The idea was to create image templates of sections within the loaded game to do a pattern match within the image. I created a solution where I loaded up the game from within the lobby, captured a screenshot after the expected SLA. I then converted the image and the template I would like to match to Bitmap.

This was followed by converting the images to grey scale. There was still one problem the image was not at the right scale to do a successful comparison. So, I resized the images to match in scale. Finally, I compared the image template to the image to match the pattern of the template within a range of 90% match.
